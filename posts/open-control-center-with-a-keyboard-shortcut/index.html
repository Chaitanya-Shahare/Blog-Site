Open Control Center on Mac With a Keyboard Shortcut

Posted by Chaitanya Shahare on Sat, Oct 21, 2023

Looking to boost your Mac’s functionality with a quick shortcut? I’ve got you covered! Learn how to open Control Center in macOS effortlessly by using Fn + C. It’s a time-saving trick that grants you instant access to key settings and features. Here’s how:

  1. Locate the ‘Fn’ Key: Find the ‘Fn’ key on your Mac’s keyboard. It’s usually in the lower-left corner.

  2. Press ‘Fn + C’: With your ‘Fn’ key identified, simply press it simultaneously with the ‘C’ key.

  3. Control Center, Engaged: Your Mac’s Control Center will pop up on the right side of your screen, granting you swift control over brightness, volume, Wi-Fi, and more.

And there you have it! With Fn + C, you’ve unlocked a convenient shortcut to enhance your Mac experience. No more navigating menus - just quick and easy access to the settings you need. Happy Mac-ing!
